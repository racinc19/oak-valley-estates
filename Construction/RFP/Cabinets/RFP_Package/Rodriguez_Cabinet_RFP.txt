SPECIFICATIONS
----------------
Construction
  • Cabinet boxes: plywood (or equivalent per approved shop drawings), per industry standard for custom residential work.
  • Drawer boxes: dovetail construction unless otherwise approved.

Door / drawer faces — OWNER SELECTION (basis of design)
  • Style: Flat slab (flush). No raised-panel or shaker unless revised in writing.
  • Finish: High-pressure laminate or approved equivalent, COLOR: Dover White, MATTE sheen only.
  • No high-gloss or mirror finish. No sprayed lacquer or site-applied paint as the primary exposed cabinet face finish.
  • Grain / appearance: Solid color or non-directional; no horizontal grain pattern on exposed faces.
  • Bidders shall submit finish samples or manufacturer data for Dover White matte (or approved equal) with the bid or at shop drawing submittal.

Hardware
  • Soft-close hinges and full-extension soft-close drawer slides throughout unless detail drawings require otherwise.
  • Pulls and knobs: Furnished and installed by cabinet contractor unless owner supplies specialty items (state in bid if allowance required).

Coordination
  • All dimensions, appliance openings, and panel sizes shall be verified against appliance specifications and architectural plans prior to fabrication.
