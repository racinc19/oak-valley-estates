Change Summary: Modify the solar fence panel orientation from upright (portrait) to laid down (landscape). Panels will be rotated 90° so the long dimension runs horizontally along the fence line.
Scope of Change
  • Rotate EG-NT66 705W panels from vertical (portrait) to horizontal (landscape) mounting.
  • Adjust frame structure: 2×1 tube top/bottom rails and 2×2 vertical posts to accommodate landscape panel dimensions (2384mm × 1303mm laid down).
  • Revise diagonal bracing (3/4×3/4×1/4 angle) as required for new orientation.
  • Update junction box / cable routing — conductors on long edges (top and bottom rails) run full length to combiner at vault end.
  • Coordination with electrical scope: DC conductor routing, combiner location, and conduit stub-ups remain per existing design unless otherwise revised.
Full Fence Elevation
Panel Assembly — Laid Down (landscape)
Technical Notes
  • Panel dimensions (landscape): 2384mm (93.86") × 1303mm (51.30") × 33mm frame.
  • 30 panels in one straight line; ~71.5m (234.6') total horizontal run.
  • Bifacial panels (BF) — East/West facing benefits from rear-side irradiance when laid down.
