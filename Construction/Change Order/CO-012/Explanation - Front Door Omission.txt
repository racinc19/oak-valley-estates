EXPLANATION — Front Entry & Casita Door Changes
================================================
For use when presenting CO-012 to the owner.

---

WHAT HAPPENED
-------------
1. FRONT DOOR: The front entry door (Main Entry / Foyer Exterior, D-ML-01) was in the plans but was left out of our door scope and subcontract. That's our error — we missed it in takeoff. The door costs $5,450 (supply, install, and hardware).

  • Owner add:               $2,725

CASITA DOOR: Upgrade from original spec:

  • Owner add:               $3,190 − original

We're not asking you to pay for our front-door mistake in full. We're owning half. The Casita is a straightforward upgrade — you pay the delta only.
