1. SCOPE OF WORK

Supply and install **motorized blinds** (motors and blinds) at all openings per suggested scope above. Scope includes:

• Furnish and install motorized blinds at all windows/glass indicated on A504 - Glass. Dimensions and counts from the drawing; no field measurement before bid.
• Installation: appropriate mounting per opening; all hardware, brackets, headrails, and motors included.
• Removal of packaging and disposal; leave openings clean and operational.
• Installation to be scheduled after interior trim and paint are complete.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2. WHAT TO INCLUDE IN YOUR BID (material, blackout, etc.)

We need **one clear price** from the drawing. To avoid back-and-forth:

• **State exactly what you’re bidding:** One material type (e.g. cellular, roller, faux-wood — your standard). One opacity (e.g. light filtering; or state “light filtering standard, blackout available as alternate”).
• **One base lump sum or unit price** for that scope. If blackout (or different material) changes the price, give it as a **separate line item or alternate** (e.g. “Blackout add $___ per opening” or “Alternate: blackout throughout $___”).
• Color/finish: selected by Owner after award; state options in the proposal.

We’ll decide material/blackout once we have your number. Your bid shall clearly state: product line, what’s included in the base price (material + opacity), and any alternates.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
