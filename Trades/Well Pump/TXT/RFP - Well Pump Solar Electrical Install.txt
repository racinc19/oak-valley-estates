DEFINITION — SOLAR ELECTRICAL VAULT SCOPE

• **Vault**: Underground enclosure, 36" x 42", with hatch at grade. Contains: Victron MPPT charge controller, 24V battery bank (100Ah x2 in series), Victron MultiPlus-II 24/3000 2x120V. DC in from solar; AC out to VFD.
• **Already installed (do not touch)**: 705W solar panel (fence-mounted); Franklin pump, pump wire, VFD Commander Pro at wellhead.
• **Contractor electrical scope**: (1) PV combiner box at array (not in vault). (2) DC cable from array to vault. (3) All vault interior wiring and component connections. (4) AC cable from vault to VFD AC input only. (5) Commissioning and labeling.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. SCOPE OF WORK — ELECTRICAL INSTALLATION ONLY

The Owner/General Contractor requests proposals for the **electrical installation only** of the well pump solar power system (panel to VFD). All equipment is Owner-furnished. Contractor shall perform electrical labor and wiring per the attached installer reference. No structural work, no fence, no concrete — electrical only.

System flow (per reference): 705W Solar Panel (already installed at fence) → PV Combiner Box (at array) → DC cable to vault → Victron MPPT → 24V Battery Bank → Victron MultiPlus-II 24/3000 → AC cable to VFD Commander Pro (at wellhead). Franklin pump and VFD are already installed; connect to VFD AC input terminals only.

Required electrical work includes:

• **PV Combiner Box at array**: Mount PJERJZN 1-string combiner (1000VDC, 40A MCB, 20–40KA SPD, IP65) within ~10 ft of panel. MC4 from panel in; DC cable out to vault. Bond ground to array frame and GEC.
• **DC cable — panel to vault**: Run USE-2 / PV wire in conduit from combiner to vault. Route per site (existing 2" PVC Solar Conduit or as shown). Terminate at vault entry for charge controller.
• **Vault interior — wiring**: (1) Mount Victron MPPT charge controller on vault wall; wire PV input from solar DC cable, output to battery bank. (2) Place and wire 24V battery bank (100Ah x2 in series); fuse on positive; connect to controller output and to inverter DC input. (3) Mount Victron MultiPlus-II 24/3000 2x120V; DC from battery, AC output L1/L2 to run to VFD. **Wire sequence**: connect batteries to controller and inverter first; then connect PV to controller. Never connect PV before batteries.
• **AC cable — vault to VFD**: Run AC from inverter output (L1, L2, ground) to VFD Commander Pro AC input terminals only. 240V split phase. Do not open VFD or modify pump wiring. Confirm 240V across L1 and L2 before energizing VFD.
• **Commissioning**: (1) Batteries on. (2) Charge controller — confirm PV input reading. (3) Inverter on — confirm AC output voltage. (4) Confirm VFD receives power. Do not test pump — already commissioned. Document string voltage and current as requested.
• **Labeling**: Label all components and connections per reference. Leave vault and array neat and code-compliant.
