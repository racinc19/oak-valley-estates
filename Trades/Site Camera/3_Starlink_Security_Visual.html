Site Layout -- Top Down
North Pole -- Component Stack
1 -- Starlink Dish Top of pole using included mount pipe. Self-orients on first boot -- leave 15 min before touching app.
2 -- Renogy 100W Panel LINOVISION bracket on side arm. Tilt 30-35 degrees, face south. MC4 cable routes down pole to battery.
3 -- aosu Camera MorningRo clamp bracket. Below dish. Aim south.
4 -- Starlink Battery Clamp to pole at 5-6 ft height. MC4 from solar panel into battery solar input. Starlink cable into battery AC output.
Wire sequence Connect battery before solar panel input. Never reverse.
100W Panel -> Starlink Battery -> Starlink Dish -> WiFi to 4 Cameras
Camera Poles -- South / East / West (all identical)
South Pole Camera at 10-11 ft on MorningRo clamp.
Aim: NORTH
Camera has built-in solar panel -- no wiring needed.
East Pole Camera at 10-11 ft on MorningRo clamp.
Aim: WEST
Camera has built-in solar panel -- no wiring needed.
West Pole Camera at 10-11 ft on MorningRo clamp.
Aim: EAST
Camera has built-in solar panel -- no wiring needed.
All poles -- concrete 18" diameter hole, 36" deep. 2 bags 80 lb concrete per pole. Plumb before pour. 24 hrs cure before mounting hardware.
Camera app setup -- all 4 Power each camera on. Download aosu app. Scan QR on camera body. Connect to Starlink WiFi on 2.4GHz band. Confirm live feed before locking final aim.
Starlink WiFi must be live first Get Starlink dish powered and internet confirmed before attempting camera setup. All 4 cameras connect wirelessly to the north pole Starlink.
