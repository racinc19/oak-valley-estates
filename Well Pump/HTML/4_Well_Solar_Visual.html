System Flow -- Panel to VFD
Vault Interior -- Component Layout
Victron MPPT Charge Controller Mount on vault wall. Wire PV input from solar DC cable. Wire output to battery bank. Batteries first -- then PV. Never reverse.
Battery Bank -- 24V / 100Ah x2 Two batteries wired in series for 24V. Connect to charge controller output and inverter DC input. Fuse on positive terminal.
Victron MultiPlus-II 24/3000 2x120V Split phase output -- L1 + L2 = 240V to VFD. DC input from battery bank. AC output to VFD Commander Pro AC input terminals only.
PV Combiner Box -- at array (not in vault) PJERJZN 1-string unit. Fuse + 40A DC breaker + SPD lightning arrestor all-in-one. Mounted at panel, not in vault. Bond ground to GEC.
Wiring -- Key Rules
1 -- PV combiner box at array PJERJZN 1-string combiner box (1000VDC, 40A MCB, 20-40KA SPD). Combines fuse, DC disconnect, and lightning arrestor in one IP65 weatherproof unit. Mount within 10 ft of panel. MC4 in from panel, DC cable out to vault. Bond ground terminal to array frame and GEC.
2 -- Wire sequence inside vault (1) Connect batteries to charge controller. (2) Connect batteries to inverter DC input. (3) Connect PV solar cable to charge controller. Never connect PV before batteries.
3 -- Victron MultiPlus-II 24/3000 2x120V AC output to VFD Wire L1 and L2 from inverter AC output to VFD Commander Pro AC input terminals. This gives 240V split phase. AC input terminals only -- do not open VFD beyond input wiring. Confirm 240V across L1 and L2 before energizing VFD.
DO NOT touch the VFD internals Franklin pump, pump wire, and VFD Commander Pro are already installed and running. Connect to AC input terminals only.
Commissioning sequence (1) Batteries on. (2) Charge controller -- confirm PV input reading. (3) Inverter on -- confirm AC output voltage. (4) Confirm VFD receives power. Do not test pump -- already commissioned.
