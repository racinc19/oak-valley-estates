1. SCOPE OF WORK — INSTALLATION ONLY

The Owner/General Contractor requests proposals for the **installation only** of the site camera and Starlink/solar system. All equipment (cameras, poles, Starlink, solar panels, battery, brackets, concrete, etc.) is Owner-furnished or already specified. Contractor shall perform labor and install per the attached installer reference documents.

Required work includes:

• **Five (5) poles**: North pole (Starlink + solar + camera); South, East, West poles (camera only). Layout per site diagram in reference doc.
• **Concrete**: 18" diameter hole, 36" deep, per pole. 2 bags 80 lb concrete per pole. Plumb before pour. 24 hrs cure before mounting hardware.
• **North pole (12 ft)**: Install per component stack in reference — (1) Starlink dish at top with included mount pipe; (2) Renogy 100W panel on LINOVISION bracket, side arm, tilt 30–35° south; (3) aosu camera on MorningRo clamp, aim south; (4) Starlink battery at 5–6 ft. Wire sequence: connect battery before solar panel input (never reverse). MC4 from panel to battery; Starlink cable to battery AC output.
• **South / East / West poles (10–11 ft)**: Camera only. Mount aosu camera at 10–11 ft on MorningRo clamp. South pole camera aims NORTH; East aims WEST; West aims EAST. Cameras have built-in solar — no wiring to poles.
• **Camera app setup**: Power each of the 4 cameras; download aosu app; scan QR on camera; connect each to Starlink WiFi (2.4 GHz). Confirm live feed before locking final aim. Starlink must be powered and internet live before camera setup.
• Coordination: Install per reference docs. Owner or GC will have equipment on site. Contractor to confirm sequence (e.g. Starlink live first, then cameras).
